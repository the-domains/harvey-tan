The Harley-Davidson lifestyle to arrive in Hong Kong 2015

The Harley-Davidson(r) lifestyle to arrive in Hong Kong 2015Blue Stone Management is currently working with the new Harley-Davidson(r) dealer to Hong Kong to develop and deliver upon a unique and complete full service dealership which will include a motorcycle showroom, clothing and parts departments, service workshop and customer lounge. Club events and group ride outs will ensue, bringing the personal expression and creativity that permeates the Harley-Davidson culture to Hong Kong.
